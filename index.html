My name is Madisen Castaneda and I'm a fifth year art student. I was born and raised in San Jose, CA and I'm a second generation Mexican. I specialize in digital art and focus on character design or just making fanart of whatever I'm into at the time.

My art is heavily influenced by my interests and I often find inspriration from them. I'm a big fan of comics, anime and manga, fiction, and fantasy. I also love listening to indie/alternative rock . Some of my favorite artists are Mother Mother, Sufjan Stevens, and SALES. Some medias that I really enjoy are Homestuck, Overlord, and the Jurassic Park Trilogy.
