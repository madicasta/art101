Madisen Correa Castaneda

Madisen Castaneda is a Mexican American artist based in San Jose, CA. She has been making art for the past five years, primarily working digitally with programs Procreate on Ipad and Paint Tool Sai on PC. She also loves working other forms of art, especially ceramics and with charcoal. Originally, Madisenn started off as a self taught artist and later became an art student at San Jose City Community College, then transfered to UC Santa Cruz.

From a young age, Madisen has been infatuated with fantasy worlds and tales from different times. This sparked her to create concept art of characters and environments. Much of her work has drawn heavy inspriation from media like anime, comics, and indie/alternative music.
